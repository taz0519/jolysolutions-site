About Joly Solutions

Joly Solutions was created to provide independent, technically grounded advisory support where energy, infrastructure, and continuity decisions have material consequence for operations, governance, and reputation.

When energy and infrastructure decisions become institutional risks

Many organizations now face aging infrastructure under capital constraints, energy programs and platforms that no longer meet security or governance expectations, and growing exposure to disruption and audit. These are board-level issues, not just technical projects.

The issue is rarely a lack of technology or effort. It is a lack of integrated perspective across engineering, operations, governance, and risk. That is where we focus.

Joly Solutions also contributes to sector-wide collaboration through initiatives such as the Ontario Energy Symposium (OECA) on January 14, bringing utilities, public institutions, and policy leaders together around practical, risk-informed pathways forward.

Portfolio-scale strategy, program renewal, resilience planning, and governance support. Vendor-agnostic, outcome-focused, and built to stand up to scrutiny.

A different way of seeing risk

Most advisors approach energy and infrastructure through a single lens (optimization, compliance, or policy). Our work is informed by accountability across operations, regulated environments, and executive governance.

  • Identify failure modes early
  • Challenge assumptions constructively
  • Design solutions that survive audit, implementation, and leadership change
