About Joly Solutions

Joly Solutions is an independent engineering and operational advisory focused on environments where energy, infrastructure, and continuity decisions carry material consequence. The firm operates at the intersection of technical reality, operational accountability, and board oversight.

Engagements are structured to be vendor-agnostic, transparent, and aligned with recognized standards, ensuring decisions are both practical and defensible over time.

Board and governance

Joly Solutions is designed to support organizations where board-level visibility into technical risk is essential. An independent governance and advisory structure allows the firm to:

  • Support committees responsible for oversight and assurance
  • Align technical pathways with enterprise risk frameworks
