Joly Solutions

Joly Solutions provides independent engineering and operational advisory for assets and programs that cannot fail quietly – where downtime, non-compliance, or platform failure carries disproportionate consequence.

Work focuses on energy, metering, and infrastructure programs that must balance capital limits, regulatory expectations, and real operating constraints.

Core capabilities

Energy solutions and resilience planning

Portfolio-scale strategy and program renewal, including evolution of legacy energy platforms, capital-aware transition pathways, and integration with asset management and continuity planning.

Regulated food and critical production environments

Engineering and operational advisory for facilities where downtime, contamination, or non-compliance carry outsized risk, aligning production reality, biosecurity, and energy reliability.

Governance, board oversight, and enterprise risk

Independent technical governance for Boards and Executives, including risk assessments, business continuity planning, and assurance frameworks aligned with ISO and sector guidance.
