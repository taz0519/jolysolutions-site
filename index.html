About Joly Solutions

Joly Solutions was created to provide independent, technically grounded advisory support where energy, infrastructure, and continuity decisions have material consequence for operations, governance, and reputation. The focus is on programs and portfolios that must stand up to audit, public scrutiny, and leadership change.

When energy and infrastructure decisions become institutional risks

Many organizations now face aging infrastructure under capital constraints; energy platforms that no longer meet security, data, or governance expectations; and growing exposure to disruption and audit. These are not just technical projects – they are board-level issues.

Joly Solutions also contributes to sector-wide collaboration through initiatives such as the Ontario Energy Symposium (OECA) on January 14 , bringing utilities, public institutions, and policy leaders together around practical, risk-informed pathways forward.
