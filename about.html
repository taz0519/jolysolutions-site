About Joly Solutions

Joly Solutions was created to provide independent, technically grounded advisory support where energy, infrastructure, and continuity decisions have material consequence for operations, governance, and reputation.

Company profile

The firm operates as an independent advisory practice, focused on complex, regulated, or high-consequence environments. Assignments typically span portfolio-scale energy strategy, program renewal, and risk-informed infrastructure planning — with a deliberate alignment to board and executive accountability structures.

Joly Solutions maintains a vendor-agnostic stance and works with specialist partners as needed, while preserving independence and clarity of accountability for recommendations.

Board orientation and governance

The firm is structured to support organizations where board and committee visibility into technical risk is essential. Engagements are framed to:

  • Align engineering and operational assessments with enterprise risk frameworks
  • Provide clear, auditable documentation of assumptions, trade-offs, and decisions
  • Support board, audit, and risk committees with concise technical synthesis
